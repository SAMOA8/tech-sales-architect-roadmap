# Recommended Reading

## Books

### Cloud Security
- *AWS Security* by Dylan Shields — practical AWS security
- *Cloud Native Security* by Chris Binnie & Rory McCune
- *Practical Cloud Security* by Chris Dotson

### Sales Engineering
- *Mastering Technical Sales* by John Care & Aron Bohlig — the bible for SEs
- *The Trusted Advisor* by David Maister
- *To Sell Is Human* by Daniel Pink

### Business Acumen
- *The Lean Startup* by Eric Ries
- *Crossing the Chasm* by Geoffrey Moore
- *Influence* by Robert Cialdini

### AI & Security
- *Adversarial Machine Learning* by Joseph et al.
- *AI Snake Oil* by Arvind Narayanan & Sayash Kapoor
