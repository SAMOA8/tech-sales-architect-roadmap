## Why a Tech Sales Architect?

Tech Sales Architects bridge the gap between deep technical capability and business outcomes. As cloud and AI rapidly converge, the most valuable Sales Architects in 2026 will be those who can:

- Explain **AWS security services** to a CISO
- Build a **business case** for AI adoption to a CFO
- Address **compliance and regulatory** concerns from legal teams
- Demonstrate **hands-on competence** to engineering teams

This roadmap is designed to make me competent in all four within 30 days.
