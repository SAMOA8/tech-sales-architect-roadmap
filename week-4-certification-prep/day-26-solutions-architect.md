- Focus: design resilient, performant, secure, cost-optimized architectures

## Security-Relevant Domains

- EC2 types and purchasing options

- CloudFront, Route 53, ALB vs NLB
- EBS vs EFS vs S3 — when to use each
- Multi-AZ vs Multi-Region patterns
- Auto Scaling Groups, Launch Templates
