- Types of ML: supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement
- Neural networks, deep learning, LLMs (conceptual)
- **Bedrock:** models, agents, knowledge bases, guardrails
- **Responsible AI:** fairness, bias, explainability, privacy, safety
- **AI Security:** prompt injection, data poisoning, model theft
- SageMaker key components and use cases
- Amazon Q (Business vs Developer)
- Cost considerations: tokens, training time, inference pricing
